Role & Responsibilities
  • Full operational authority across UK and EU region — inbound passengers, contracting, DMC management, HR and P&L

How UK & India Work Together — The 5-Step Flow
1
UK Sets Strategy
Ajay (UK) defines Europe product strategy, contracts hotels & suppliers, sets itinerary concepts & pricing
2
Mumbai Builds
Ronak & Amit build the itineraries, create product content, and brief the tech team on visuals
3
UK Signs Off
UK approves all products and content. Nothing goes live without COO Europe sign-off
4
Disha Loads
Disha's 33-person team loads all approved product into TripFactory — rates, itineraries, hotels, visuals
5
Amir's Team Sells
18-strong dedicated Europe sales & agent team in Mumbai converts enquiries and manages all Europe bookings
